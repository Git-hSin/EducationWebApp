What nuances can data uncover about education?

Introduction

Our project aims to demonstrate the power of data in terms of what can be learned in education.

This dashboard was designed to provide exploratory analysis using multiple data sources in order to summarize our findings. These data sources include but are not limited to Amazon, the New York Times Best Sellers, The US Department of Education, and the US Census.

With these sources, we use a regression analysis modeler in our dashboard to help us identify any correlations that might exist using variables we are curious about.
